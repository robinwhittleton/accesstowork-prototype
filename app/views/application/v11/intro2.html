2. Eligibility

You can only apply for an Access to Work grant if you live and work in England, Scotland or Wales. There's a different system in Northern Ireland.

Your condition

Your disability or health condition must affect your ability to do your job or mean you have to pay work-related costs. For example, if you need to buy computer equipment or have to take taxis because you canâ€™t use public transport.

Other benefits

You might not get a grant if you already get any of:

  • Incapacity Benefit
  • Job Seekers Allowance
  • Employment and Support Allowance
  • Severe Disability Allowance
  • Income Support
  • National Insurance Credits

Changing jobs

You may be able to take any special equipment that's been paid for with an Access to Work grant with you if you change jobs.

If you get a grant for travel costs you'll have to apply for a new grant if you change your place of work.
