Using email

Dealing with your Access To Work application digitally may require e-mailing your personal data outside of the Secure network used by DWP. We need to inform you of the risks associated with e-mailing outside a secure network, the primary risks are loss, manipulation and theft of emails.

If you do not accept these risks, please do not submit an application via this web page.
