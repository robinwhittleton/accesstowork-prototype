1. Overview

You can apply for an Access to Work grant if you have a disability or health condition that causes you difficulty doing your job.

The grant can be used to pay for special equipment or support to help you:

  • do work tasks, either in your current job or for a new role
  • get to and from work

It can also be used to help you in a job interview if you have communication difficulties.

You can't get a grant to help with business start-up costs.
