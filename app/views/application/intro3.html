3. What you'll get

How much you'll be offered depends on how much help you need.

A grant can pay for things like:

  • changes to equipment you have to use for your job
  • new special equipment, eg furniture or computer software
  • travel costs to and from work
  • someone to help you in your workplace, eg a support worker
  • work coaching or mentoring
  • disability awareness training for your colleagues
  • moving special equipment if you change your job

Maximum grant

The most you can get as an Access to Work grant is £40,800 per year.

If you have a grant that's worth more it'll be reduced in April 2018.
