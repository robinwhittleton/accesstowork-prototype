3. What you'll get

How much you'll be offered depends on your condition and how much help you need.

A grant can pay for things like:

  • changes to equipment you have to use for your job
  • new special equipment, eg furniture or computer software
  • travel costs to and from work
  • someone to help you in your workplace, eg a support worker
  • a support service
  • disability awareness training for your colleagues
  • someone to help you communicate at a job interview, eg a sign language interpreter
  • moving special equipment if you change your job

Changes to Access to Work

From 1 October 2015 the maximum grant that you could be offered will be £40,800 per year.

If you already have a grant or are given one before 1 October 2015 that's worth more than £40,800 it'll be reduced in April 2018.
