To get an Access to Work grant you must be:

  • 16 or over
  • have a long term disability or health condition that makes it difficult for you to do your current job or a job you're about to start
  • living and working in England, Scotland or Wales

Department for Work & Pensions will contact your employer when you apply. Your employer could have to pay some of your grant, depending on how much you get.

An assessor might have to visit you at work to make a decision.

More about long term health conditions

Your health condition or disability doesn't have to be permanent but you must either:

  • have had it for at least a year
  • expect it to last at least another 12 months from when you apply

You don't usually need to give any medical evidence of your condition as part of your application.
