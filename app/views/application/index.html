1. Overview

You can apply for an Access to Work grant if you have a long-term disability or health condition that makes it difficult for you to do your job.

The grant can pay for special equipment or support to help you:

  • do work tasks
  • get to and from work

You can't get a grant to help with business start-up costs.

Other benefits

You might not get a grant if you already get:

  • Incapacity Benefit
  • Job Seekers Allowance
  • Employment and Support Allowance
  • Severe Disability Allowance
  • Income Support
  • National Insurance Credits
