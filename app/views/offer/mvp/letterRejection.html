Dear {{ you_full_name | default("Mrs Smith") }}

You've not been awarded an Access to Work grant.

An Access to Work grant can only be used to pay for things that help you work if your problem is directly related to your condition or disability.

If the help you want isn't directly relevant to your work role or the changes needed to help you are considered 'reasonable adjustments' that your employer should make, then you aren't entitled to a grant.

If you're still having difficulty at work you can ask your manager about making reasonable adjustments to what you do or the equipment you use.

You can find out more about reasonable adjustments on GOV.UK
