3. What you'll get

How much you'll be offered depends on your condition and how much help you need.

The grant can pay for things like:

  • adaptations to equipment you have to use in your job
  • special equipment
  • travel costs to work if you can't use public transport
  • someone to help you in your workplace, eg a support worker
  • a support service, if you have a mental health condition
  • disability awareness training for your colleagues
  • someone to help you communicate at a job interview
  • moving special equipment if you change your job

Changes to Access to Work

From 1 October 2015 the maximum grant that you could be offered will be £40,800.

If you have a grant before 1 October 2015 that's worth more than £40,800 it will be reduced in April 2018.
