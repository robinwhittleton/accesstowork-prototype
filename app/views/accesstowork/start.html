Before you start

To get an Access to Work grant you must:

  • be 16 or over
  • live and work in England, Scotland or Wales
  • have a job or a job interview
  • have a long term disability or health condition

Department for Work and Pensions will contact your employer when you apply and an assessor might have to visit you at work.

About long term health conditions

Your health condition or disability doesn't have to be permanent but you must either:

  • have had it for at least a year
  • expect it to last at least another 12 months from when you apply

You don't usually need to give any medical evidence of your condition as part of your application.
